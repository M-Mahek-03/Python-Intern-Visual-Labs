M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering

Established in 1984, M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering is a premier Institute located in Maharashtra. Institute offers Degree courses including 9 UG course. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering is a premier institute for B.E. / B.Tech courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like B.E. / B.Tech with the stream of Engineering. Over the period, M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Electronics Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Automobile Engineering. Courses offered by institute are recognized by approving bodies like AICTE. M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering offered courses for 540 seats. Courses offered by the Institute are in the fee range of INR 478,772 for a total of 540 candidates. M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering provide good facilities all around the campus like Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Cafeteria, Girls Hostel, Hostel, Labs, Library, Sports Complex.

Mohammad Haji Sabo Siddik College of Engineering (MHSSCOE) is a well-known technical institute established in 1984 at Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is approved by AICTE and is affiliated to the University of Mumbai. The college offers Bachelor of Engineering programs across 7 specializations. Admission to the program is granted based on MHT-CET or JEE Mains score. Students under different branches of engineering get placed in reputed companies every year.
